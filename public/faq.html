FAQ

Official URL: https://spotzapp.app/faq

Answers to common questions about using SPOTZ and managing your account.

What is SPOTZ?

SPOTZ is a photography location discovery app for finding, saving, sharing, and discussing photo spots.

Can I add my own spots?

Yes. Signed-in users can add photo spots with images, coordinates, categories, descriptions, and best-time details.

Can other users see my spots?

Public spots and related profile details may be visible to other SPOTZ users. Review the Privacy Policy for more detail.

How do I report unsafe or inappropriate content?

Use the in-app report tools where available for spots, comments, replies, profiles, and safety concerns.

How do I delete my account?

Open SPOTZ, go to Profile, open Settings, choose Legal, then select Delete Account. The Account Deletion page explains what is deleted, anonymized, or retained.

Where can I read the legal pages?

Use the public links for the Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, Community Guidelines, Account Deletion, and Support pages.
